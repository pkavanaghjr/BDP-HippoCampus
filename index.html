Hippos On Campus

Blue Devil Productions is proud to present Hippo Campus with special guests TBA on Thursday, October 29th, 2015 in the Great hall at 8pm. Doors open at 7pm. The Great Hall is located on the upper level of the Memorial Student Center at the University of Wisconsin-Stout.

Stitches, Beans, Espo, and Turntan; these are the eclectically cool, code names for the Minnesota natives of the indie pop-rock band Hippo Campus. This young quartet has been on the rise since they formed in 2013, right around the time of their high school graduation. Their age is part of their charm; with pop-rock sounds similar to that of Vampire Weekend, the boys remember to infuse a bit of youth into their music. The band’s debut EP, ‘Bashful Creatures’ was produced by Alan Sparkhawk and was released shortly after their television debut on Conan. The band has also had performances at SXSW, Lollapalooza, and a recent set at Chipotle Cultivate Fest in Minneapolis. Hippo Campus has also recently released their second EP, ‘South.’ Beware all you bashful creatures; this show will be one for the record books. The band might even perform a few hits off their new EP. After all, they like to keep things a little mysterious…you will just have to show up to find out.

This event is sponsored by Blue Devil Productions of the University of Wisconsin-Stout. Tickets purchased in advance to this event are $5 for UW-Stout students (present UW-Stout I.D. at door) and $9 for the public. Ticket prices will be $7 for UW-Stout students and $11 for the public the day of show. Tickets are available at tickets.uwstout.edu. Please call (715)-232-2432 or go to bdp.uwstout.edu for more information.

Accessibility: For event guests with special needs, requiring alternative accommodations, please contact Emily Ascher at aschere@uwstout.edu to make arrangements.

Meet the Hippos

Enter our meet and greet contest for a chance to meet Hippo Campus! There will be 5 winners selected and each winner can bring 1 friend. Winners will get a chance to meet Hippo Campus, receive a signed poster, and take a picture with them.

To enter the meet and greet contest you must generate a meme related to Hippo Campus and submit it to BDP via email or Facebook message. We have a few images of Hippo Campus available for use, but feel free to get creative and use any imagery you like. Submissions will be posted by BDP in a photo album on Facebook for the public to vote by liking photos. Only the likes on the photos in BDP’s album will be counted. Likes on shared images and personal posts will NOT be counted, we recommend sharing the link to our Facebook album. The 5 images with the most likes will be the contest winners.

Contest closes Wednesday, October 28th at 5pm. We will notify winners via email, you MUST RESPOND to the email by Thursday, October 29th by 12pm or a new winner will be selected.
